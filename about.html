About

Hello! I am Robotics and Automation engineering undergraduate student eager to develop projects that can be used for technical as well as social purpose. Robots fascinated me since my childhood, the dream of making robots to simplify and automate tasks for me lead me to pursue my undergradute in robotics and automation. My passion has made me to realise my dream by learning about vast range of technologies right from basic electronics, controllers, processors, sensors, linux based systems to ROS, Robot kinematics, computer vision and artificial intelligence. My major area of interest has been a fusion of all these technologies which is autonomous navigation, showcased by the projects I have made and still working on. The journey has just begun, miles to go before I sleep.
